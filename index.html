BirthBeat

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information BirthBeat processes, what stays on your device, and how we keep your data handling minimal and transparent.

Effective date: February 1, 2026 Last updated: February 17, 2026 Applies to iOS and Android

In normal app use, BirthBeat does not require account registration and does not send your contraction logs to our servers. Contraction data remains on your device.

1Who We Are

BirthBeat is a contraction timing app developed and maintained as an independent project. This privacy policy describes how information is handled when you use BirthBeat on iOS or Android.

If you have privacy questions, contact details are provided in Section 10.

2Data We Process

BirthBeat is designed to collect as little data as possible.

  • Not collected by default app use: your name, email, address, contact list, photos, or your contraction history.
  • Stored on your device only: contraction timings, session history, and your app preferences.
  • Processed by third parties in limited cases: payment transaction records (Apple/Google) and technical crash diagnostics (Firebase), as described in Section 4.
Validation note: this section is aligned with the third-party section to avoid conflicting statements about crash reporting and platform processing.

3Data Stored on Your Device

BirthBeat stores the following locally on your phone and does not sync this data to our own servers:

  • Contraction start and end times
  • Calculated duration and interval values
  • Session history grouped by date
  • App settings and preferences
  • Optional due date and selected labor criteria

You can delete local data from within the app at any time through Settings > Clear All Data.

4Third-Party Services

BirthBeat uses the services below for payments, distribution, and app stability. These providers act under their own terms and privacy policies.

Service Purpose Data Scope
Apple In-App Purchase Optional tip payments on iOS Apple processes payment details and transaction metadata. We do not receive full card data.
Google Play Billing Optional tip payments on Android Google processes payment details and transaction metadata. We do not receive full card data.
Expo / EAS Build and distribution infrastructure Operational build data for app release workflows.
Google Firebase Crashlytics Crash reporting and stability monitoring May process technical diagnostics such as app version, device model, OS version, and crash stack traces. We do not intentionally include health notes or contraction logs in crash payloads.

5Legal Basis (GDPR)

For the app's local-only functionality, BirthBeat does not rely on server-side personal data processing.

  • Crash diagnostics: processed under legitimate interests to maintain reliability and user safety.
  • Payments: transaction processing is handled by Apple and Google as independent controllers under their legal bases.

Where required, we support user requests and regulator inquiries consistent with GDPR obligations.

6Your Privacy Rights

If you are in the EU/EEA or UK, you may have rights including access, correction, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.

Because BirthBeat keeps core usage data on your device, many rights are supported by design. If you submit a request, we respond within one month, subject to applicable law.

You can also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7Medical Disclaimer

BirthBeat is a general timing aid and not a medical device. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or emergency guidance.

  • Do not use BirthBeat as a substitute for professional medical advice.
  • Always follow instructions from your doctor, midwife, or emergency services.
  • If symptoms are severe or concerning, seek immediate medical care.
If you experience heavy bleeding, severe pain, reduced fetal movement, fever, or any urgent symptom, contact a healthcare professional or emergency services immediately.

8Children's Privacy

BirthBeat is intended for pregnant adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the app. We cannot recover deleted local records.

For third-party services (for example payment records or crash logs), retention periods are set by the relevant provider under its own policy.
